How Cookie Protection Works

What this extension does

This extension automatically removes unnecessary cookies that don’t contribute to your browsing experience, privacy, or security.

Some cookies are required temporarily for websites to function correctly. These are handled safely and removed when appropriate.

Clean Now vs Smart Protection

Clean Now
A manual cleanup that removes unnecessary cookies at the moment you click.

Smart Protection
Automatic background protection that continuously removes unnecessary cookies without interrupting your browsing.

Why some cookies may remain

Some cookies must remain temporarily to keep you logged in, prevent site breakage, and ensure security and stability.

These cookies are not ignored — they are removed automatically once they are no longer needed.

About “Legitimate Interest”

Some vendors operate under legitimate interest rather than consent. These are often related to analytics, security, or basic advertising infrastructure.

The extension prioritizes browser stability first and removes unnecessary cookies under legitimate interest when it is safe to do so.

Logged out of a site?

If you were logged out, it means essential session cookies were removed.

This is expected behavior for strict privacy cleanup. Whitelist controls will be available in a future update.

Status indicators

Active means protection is running normally.
Inactive means an error occurred and protection is temporarily paused.
