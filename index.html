Silent Guardian

Protects your privacy — quietly and effectively

What is Silent Guardian?

Silent Guardian is a free extension for Chrome and Edge that automatically removes unwanted cookies and trackers, while preserving the cookies you need for websites to function properly.

How it works

1. Clean Now

Instantly removes tracking and advertising cookies with a single click.

2. Smart Protection

When enabled, the extension automatically cleans cookies every 30 minutes in the background, without interrupting your browsing. The status Active/Inactive indicates if the Smart Protection is enabled or not.

3. Bulk Opt-Out

On supported websites with multiple vendors, Bulk Opt-Out helps you opt out of advertising and tracking vendors more efficiently by automating the selection process. No need to manage each vendor individually.

4. Whitelist & Blacklist

  • Whitelist: Exclude websites you never want to be cleaned (e.g. banking, email, shopping sites).
  • Blacklist: Add domains that should always be removed.

Why are some cookies kept?

Silent Guardian does not remove essential cookies required for login, active sessions, shopping carts, and your preferences. These cookies are preserved to ensure websites continue to function properly and your browsing experience remains uninterrupted.

Complete Privacy

• We do not collect any of your data.
• All operations run locally in your browser.
• No servers, analytics, or tracking are involved.
• The extension is 100% offline and private.
